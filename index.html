When do I pay my taxes? What are the taxes I am supposed to pay in the Philippines?

  • Monthly
    • BIR Form 0619E - If you are renting, paying professionals or commissions. This is due for payment/filling on or before 10 th day of the following month.
    • BIR Form 1601C - For reporting salaries of employees and their corresponding withholding tax. This is due for payment/filling on or before 10 th day of the following month.
    • BIR Form 2550M - For VAT Individual. It is computed as 12% of monthly vatable sales less 12% of vatable purchases/expenses. This is due for payment/filling on or before 20 th day of the following month.

Where do I pay my taxes?

You pay your taxes to the ADB of your local Revenue District Office. For taxpayers with branches, the taxes can be consolidated with the main office.

What expenses are deductible in my income taxes?

Expenses which are deductible are ordinary and necessary for the conduct or trade or business or profession. However, expenses which are personal to the business owners or entrepreneurs and do not contribute to earning the income are not allowed as deductions.

Do I need an accountant to pay my taxes?

No, as long as you know how to compute your tax due and the deadlines of the payment.
